=== INTEGRATION 2: BACKLINKS DATA ===

Add backlink intelligence to 
competition tab.

Endpoint:
POST https://api.dataforseo.com/v3/
backlinks/summary/live

Request:
[{
  "target": "mealcoreai.com",
  "include_subdomains": true
}]

Returns:
- referring_domains count
- backlinks total count
- domain_rank score
- referring_main_domains

Use for:
1. Competition tab — add backlink column:
   Domain | AI Score | Backlinks | DR

2. Fix Actions — citation gap:
   "Competitor has 450 referring domains.
   You have 12.
   Here are sites that link to them
   but not you: [list]"

Also use competitor backlinks:
POST /backlinks/competitors/live
{
  "targets": [
    "mealcoreai.com",
    "healthifyme.com",
    "sugarfit.com"
  ]
}

Returns domains that link to 
competitors but not to user.
These are priority citation targets.

Add to Fix Actions Week 4:
"Sites linking to your competitors
but not you:"
[List with DR scores and Submit links]
